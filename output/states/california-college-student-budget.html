FAQ: College Spending Money in California

How much spending money do most college students need in California?

Many students in California end up needing somewhere between $1376 and $2064 per month for non‑tuition costs like food, going out, transportation, and extras. Your number will be higher in big cities and lower in smaller towns.

How can I cut my monthly college budget in California?

The biggest levers are housing, food, and nightlife. Living with roommates, using a meal plan smartly, and setting a weekly going‑out limit can easily save $100–$300 per month for many students in California.

Should I work during the semester or only in the summer?

A lot of students in California work 8–15 hours per week during the semester. Use the job hours calculator on your live site to see whether it makes more sense for you to work now, save up in advance, or combine both.
